delve deep

in honor of side by side's second anniversary on may 14th, we'll be kicking off the month of with a character development challenge! we thought this challenge would be a fun way to look back on the last two years, and to celebrate everyone's writing in the process. whether you joined yesterday or have been here since the start, these prompts are designed to get you thinking about your characters, your writing, and the stories we've created together.

below are ten prompts centered around character development, each numbered and named for ease of reference. you may interpret these prompts however you wish; there is no right or wrong way to respond to a prompt. they are meant to be thought starters, so follow your heart and get creative!

to participate in the challenge, write up your responses and post them somewhere in the development forum (existing or new topics welcome). then, post in the thread below using the code provided. please only post here once - you may edit your post up until the end of the challenge on may 31st, 2024 at 11:59pm. there is no word count requirement for prompt responses, but we encourage you to delve deep!

you will be rewarded points for each prompt you respond to (per character), and an additional points prize will be given for those who complete all ten unique prompts (across characters). all participants will also receive a special 2nd anniversary badge on the account of their choice. feel free to participate with as many or as little characters as you'd like. we can't wait to see what you all come up with!

the prompts

  • PROMPT 001 THE FUTURE
    • where does your character see themselves in five years? does this differ from where you, the player, see them?
  • PROMPT 002 THE MOTIVE
    • how have your character’s aspirations and motivations changed since joining the site? what does your character want?
  • PROMPT 003 THE QUILL
    • what are some of your favorite things you’ve written for this character? what was the most fun to write? the hardest?
  • PROMPT 004THE STORYBOOK
    • what are some of your character’s most treasured memories from the past two years? what moments (from a thread, comm, etc.) will live on as fun stories their friends/family tell at social gatherings?
  • PROMPT 005 THE MUSE
    • what was the inspiration for this character? walk us through your character creation process. in what ways has the character evolved from how you originally imagined them?
  • PROMPT 006 THE MIRROR
    • how is your character’s value system (personal philosophy, religious doctrine, cultural norms, etc.) different from or similar to your own? how does it feel to write from this perspective?
  • PROMPT 007 THE GHOST
    • what is the biggest "what-if" moment in your character’s life? what’s something that keeps them up at night?
  • PROMPT 008 THE LIE
    • what’s something your character wholeheartedly believes that is not true? this could be a flawed philosophy, a misconception about themselves, or something else entirely.
  • PROMPT 009 THE ORNAMENT
    • what does your character always carry with them? do they have any special items or mementos that hold a significant meaning for them?
  • PROMPT 010 THE LETTER
    • have your character write a letter to their past self (either from the start of their time on the site or further back). what advice do they have to give?
